About Me

I’m a junior majoring in Computer Science at Cornell University, where I’m fortunate to be advised by Prof. Claire Cardie. I’ve also collaborated with Prof. Thorsten Joachims and PhD student Zhaolin Gao on building a demo system for LLM-based academic review generation.


Research

My research focuses on improving reasoning, factuality, and adaptability in large language models (LLMs). I’m currently developing frameworks to enhance LLMs' multi-hop reasoning performance and exploring techniques for automated dataset curation aimed at enabling self-evolving LLMs. Additionally, I’m investigating the application of LLMs for generating lyrics sensitive to cultural and temporal contexts.
